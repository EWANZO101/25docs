Troubleshooting Guide

Here are some common issues and how to resolve them:

  • Cannot Connect to Server: Ensure that your network connection is stable and try restarting your router.
  • Login Issues: Double-check your username and password. If you forgot your password, reset it here.
  • Slow Website: Clear your browser’s cache and check your hosting plan’s resource limits.
  • Email Not Sending: Verify your email client settings and ensure that no outbound restrictions are in place.

For further assistance, contact support.
