Billing, Payments, and Invoices

Managing Your Billing

Our service provides an easy way to manage your billing information. You can update your billing address, view your billing history, and manage payment methods directly through your account dashboard.

Making Payments

You can make payments through our secure portal. Acceptable payment methods include credit/debit cards and PayPal. To make a payment, log into your account, navigate to the payments section, and follow the prompts.

Viewing Invoices

Invoices for your services are generated automatically at the end of each billing cycle. You can view and download your invoices from the billing section of your account. Invoices are available in PDF format for your records.

Contact Support

If you have any questions regarding billing, payments, or invoices, feel free to contact our support team. You can reach us via the support ticket system or by emailing support@swiftpeakhosting.co.uk.
