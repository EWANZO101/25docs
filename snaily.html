Adding Discord Integration

  1. SSH into your server.
  2. Run the following commands: 
    sudo -s
# Enter your password
cd /home/
cd snaily-cadv4
nano .env

At the bottom of the .env file, add the Discord integration template:

DISCORD_BOT_TOKEN=""
DISCORD_SERVER_ID=""
DISCORD_CLIENT_ID=""
DISCORD_CLIENT_SECRET=""

Fill in the required details, save by pressing Ctrl + S, and exit with Ctrl + X.

Finally, restart your CAD via our dashboard, and the Discord integration will be active!
