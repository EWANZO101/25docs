Important Setup Confirmation

Before we proceed with your SnailyCAD setup, it’s essential to ensure that all steps in our process have been completed.

  • Your SnailyCAD order will take 12–24 hours to activate. Initial errors are normal during this time.
  • If you are using a Swift Peak Hosting subdomain (e.g., LARP.snailycad.co.uk), your setup will be expedited.
  • Cloudflare is a mandatory requirement for hosting services with Swift Peak Hosting.

After confirmation, you’ll be redirected to a checklist to finalize your setup and ensure smooth activation of your CAD.

If you encounter any issues or do not receive your CAD within the specified timeframe, please contact us via our Contact Us page for assistance.
