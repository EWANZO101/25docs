Frequently Asked Questions

What is Swift Peak Hosting?

Swift Peak Hosting provides hosting services and integrations for SnailyCAD to FiveM frameworks like QBCore and ESX, along with other related services.

How can I contact support?

You can reach out to our support team by logging into your account and submitting a ticket via the client area. Alternatively, you can email us or use live chat (if available).

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other supported payment gateways. You can view the payment options at checkout.

How do I pay my invoice?

Log in to your account, navigate to the Billing section, and click on the unpaid invoice to complete the payment.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are subject to our refund policy. Please review our Terms of Service for details on eligibility.

What services do you offer?

We offer hosting for FiveM servers, integrations for SnailyCAD, and additional tools for QBCore and ESX frameworks.

How do I upgrade or downgrade my service?

Log in to your client area, navigate to your active service, and select the Upgrade/Downgrade option. Any pricing adjustments will be reflected in your invoice.

How do I cancel my service?

To cancel a service, log in to your account, go to My Services, select the service you want to cancel, and click on Request Cancellation.
